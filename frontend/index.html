Our Story

Built for Bharat's Farmers

🌾
₹50,000 Cr
annual crop losses due to undetected diseases in India

India has over 146 million farming families, yet most lack access to timely agronomic expertise. Crop diseases alone cause 20–40% yield loss annually. CropDr. was built by a team of engineers and agronomists who believe that AI should serve those who feed the nation.

We combine computer vision, real-time weather data, and agricultural science to give every farmer — from Punjab to Tamil Nadu — the power of an expert in their pocket, in their language, for free.

🎯
Accuracy First
98% detection accuracy across 120+ diseases, validated with ICAR datasets.

Sustainable Agriculture
We always recommend organic treatments first, reducing chemical dependence.
